cronota: A stopwatch with notes.

Type or paste your "notes" to see while speaking, then click "Start" button to start the stopwatch.
Each line from your notes and the time you spend for it will be logged by clicking "Next" button.

Pasting your notes is recommended because reloading this page erases your notes.
